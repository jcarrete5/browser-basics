Managing your life

The internet comes in very handy when it comes to managing and maintaining certain elements in your life

Online Banking:

Most major banks allow online banking. Many major functions such as viewing monthly statements, making deposits, withdrawals, transferring money from one account to another and other common bank activities. The details of the same differ slightly for every bank but the core functionalities remain similar. The website for your bank can be found by searching “ *your bank name* online” on a search engine like www.google.com

Paying your Bills:

Often, a lot of bills can be paid online or even be set-up to automatically deducted from your bank account so that you don’t have to worry about paying a particular bill each month. You can set this up through your bank’s website and maybe through the website of the organization you’re paying. For more information, you can call the organization directly.

Online Shopping:

There are several e-shops set up online where you can pay for and buy things online which will be delivered to your doorstep. These things include electronics, groceries, furniture, books, and much more. E-Commerce is a very big part of the internet world where transactions are conducted online and the products can either be availed online or delivered right to your doorstep. One such website is www.amazon.com.

Word of Caution:

Only use trusted and safe websites to do online transactions. If you enter your details in a suspicious website, you risk someone stealing your bank details which will give them access to your account. This would mean that they can use your account details to spend money online without your knowledge.

Creating Documents, Presentations and Excel sheets:

There are websites online which help you create documents, presentations and excel sheets for free. One such (linked with your google account) is www.google.com/docs. This is where you can create word documents for free online. You can write papers, create resumes, create memo’s and much more using this software. Another feature of this is that you can access these files on any other computer connected to the internet by logging into your google account and going to the same website.

Word of Caution:

Always sign out after you’ve finished your work as if someone else access the computer with your account signed in, they will be able to view and edit all the documents you have created.

Calendar/Planning tools:

The internet also has many tools/free online services which help you plan, schedule, and manage your day to day schedule. One such website is calendar.google.com. This is a service offered by Google which is linked to the email address you created in the eMail module. You can click on the “Create” button to add tasks you need to do throughout the day. Whether you have a dinner with friends, travelling somewhere, or just things you need to do at a certain time of the day, you can schedule them using this website.

Applying for Jobs:

Most companies today accept job applications online and will get back to you via email. With the ever-modernising world, the companies around the world have adopted the system of managing job applications via their respective websites. There are 2 ways you can go about applying for jobs.

You can apply for jobs directly from the company’s website. You can access it by searching the company’s name (ensure you go to the correct and official website) and from there, you can submit your resume online to apply and submit an application. The company will reach out to you via email if they’re interested and if you don’t get a response, move on to another.

There are websites where companies collectively post jobs on it to streamline the job application process. You can filter the jobs (using the filter button) to find the job you want, in the area you want, and simplify the process. Hence, instead of submitting your resume to 25 different companies for 25 different applications, you’re uploading only 1 resume and at the click of a button, sending it to 25 different companies. One such example of a job-search website is www.indeed.com, the #1 job site in the world.

Word of Caution:

Only use reliable websites to apply for jobs. The wrong websites will steal your information with malicious intent, you could become a victim of identity theft.
