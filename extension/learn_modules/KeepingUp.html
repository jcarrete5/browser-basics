Keeping up with World Affairs

Reading the News

The internet makes the world a smaller place. There are a lot of resources available online for you to stay updated with events happening locally, nationally and globally. Watching the News

The internet also provides us the resources to watch the news online. The same websites (BBC, CNN, Fox News and others) give you the ability to watch clips of the news associated to each news article.

Word of Caution:

Because of the sheer number of news sites and headlines available through the internet, there are some news sources that project false, heavily biased and provocative news to attract more people on the internet. It is a good habit to have to fact check the news we read using other sources to get a better and a more clearer picture of the same.
