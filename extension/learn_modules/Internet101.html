Internet 101 : The First Step

Congratulations on taking your first step into learning how to surf the internet! The internet is a wonderous place which connects all computers from all around the world together on a common network. It is the biggest contributor to why the world is a smaller place today!

What is Internet?

The internet is a global system of interconnected computer networks that use the Internet Protocol to link devices worldwide. In simpler terms, it is a network of computers around the world which are capable of talking to each other.

What all can you do on the internet?

The internet carries a vast range of information, functionality, resources and services. Some of it's function include (but aren't limited to):
- Communicating and Connecting with people
- Receiving updates from around the world
- Tools for managing life and aspects of it
- Researching Information on a certain topic
- Entertainment
