Connecting you with People

Social media is the most popular and maybe the most effective form of connecting with family, friends and other people.There are several different web platforms over which people do so. Each platform has a different perceptions all over the world

For example, Facebook is used at a more informal level to stay connected and updated with friends and acquaintances. People post status updates, life events, pictures of events and other things you’d want to talk about with friends and family.

LinkedIn is a social media platforms for professionals, professional organizations and those looking to enter a profession/career. It has a lot of functionality poised towards helping you network, develop professional skills and explore hidden professional opportunities for jobs and careers. It’s free, a means for you to stay connected with former colleagues, stay updated with industry specific news and LinkedIn is used by recruiters to access the hidden job market.

Social Media is a very powerful tool when used properly. It is your personal footprint that you leave on the internet. Everything that you do on the internet leaves a footprint. Hence, you need to be careful with what you put on it.

Word of Caution:

Social Media is a place where anyone can look up and find out about anyone. Therefore, you must be careful as to what information you put on it. Social media platforms are a very powerful tool and can be a big liability if not used properly. What goes on the internet stays on there forever in the form of data somewhere.

With informal platforms like facebook, what you put on there is publically available for anyone and everyone. Hence, your private information (passwords, credit card details, etc) must never be shared over the same.
